LINEAR EQUATIONS

An equation that has maximum degree one is known as Linear Equations. the graph of it is a straight line.

For example:

  • y = 8x - 2
  • ax1 + bx2 - cx3 = 9

are some of the examples of a linear equations in two and three variables.

Slope of the intersection of two linear equations

y1 = mx1 + a and
y2 = mx2 + b
For the points P(x1, y1) and Q(x2, y2) is:

m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)
